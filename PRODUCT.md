# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Startup founders, SMB owners, enterprise teams, agencies, and SaaS entrepreneurs who need custom software built. They arrive with a problem or an idea and are evaluating agencies to build it. They're busy, results-oriented, and care about business outcomes over technical detail.

## Product Purpose

Gawa Developer is a software development agency whose website has one job: convert visitors into qualified project leads. Every section exists to move a visitor one step closer to submitting a project inquiry. Success is a filled contact form.

## Brand Personality

Confident, capable, trustworthy. The agency demonstrates mastery through clarity, not through buzzwords. Tone is direct and professional — "we build software that works, here's why you should hire us." No hedging, no corporate filler.

## Anti-references

- Gradient-heavy SaaS templates with hero-metric stat dashboards
- Overcrowded agency sites that list 40 services and say nothing
- Developer portfolio sites that prioritize dark aesthetics over conversion
- Sites that bury the primary CTA behind content walls or micro-conversions

## Design Principles

1. **Conversion first**: Every design decision should answer "does this help a visitor become a lead?" Decoration that doesn't serve that goal is cost, not value.
2. **Confident restraint**: Clean and minimal, but deliberate. Whitespace is chosen, not defaulted to. Emptiness must earn its place.
3. **Outcomes over process**: Copy and visuals emphasize what clients get (working software, business growth, reliability) not internal methods or team size.
4. **Trust through professionalism**: Typography, spacing, and color signal seriousness. The site should feel like the agency it's selling.
5. **Performance is the product**: A site claiming to build fast software must itself be fast. Core Web Vitals are a brand statement.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Touch targets ≥ 48×48px. Semantic HTML throughout. No FOIT/FOUT on font load. Reduced-motion alternatives required for all animations.
